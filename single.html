What is Panchakarma..........


Panchakarma is one of the most well-known parts about Ayurveda. It is a five-step, total mind-body rejuvenation experience that comprises of herbal oil massages, steam baths, cleansing enemas, a healing kitchari diet and other purifying practices.


Panchakarma is amazing because it eliminate toxins from the system, rebalance the Doshas, heal countless ailments and renew a sense of clarity and inner-peace.


However, it isn’t like a normal spa holiday with poolside massages and green juices. It is a mind-body experience that will ooze toxins out of all orifices of your body, nose and ears included.


The five traditional therapies of Panchakarma are:

  1. Nasya
  2. Vaman
  3. Virechana
  4. Vasthi
  5. Raktha Moksha
